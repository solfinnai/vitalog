Multimodal AI

Photo + voice + text. One entry.

VitaLog uses the same AI that understands images, text, and speech — all in a single request. Snap a photo of a rash, then describe how it feels. The AI correlates what it sees with what you said.

  • 📷
    AI image analysis — Describes what it observes: size, color, shape, texture. Never diagnoses — just careful observation.
  • 🔗

  • 💊
    Reads medication labels — Snap a photo of a pill bottle. AI extracts drug name, dosage, and manufacturer.
